Sri Poornima Enterprises facilitates a robust wholesale staple distribution network at the core of Oltowns operations. With deep roots in the agricultural sector, Oltowns acts as a vital link between farmers and retailers, ensuring a seamless supply chain for essential commodities. By collecting and distributing staples directly from the source, Oltowns guarantees the freshness and authenticity of its products while supporting local farming communities.

Our Story

Founded in 2017 by Mr. Shankar Dharman, Karunadu Products India Private Limited has quickly established itself as a pioneer in the retail industry. Headquartered in RT Nagar, Bangalore, we have grown from a simple idea to a dynamic force in the market, driven by our commitment to making a positive difference in the lives of Indian consumers.

At Karunadu, we believe that success is not achieved in isolation. Our journey is intertwined with the progress of our customers, partners, and communities. We take pride in being part of India's evolving retail landscape and are excited about the many chapters yet to unfold.

Mr. Shankar Dharman

Founder & Chairman

Our Vision

To revolutionize the retail landscape by offering innovative, sustainable, and high-quality products and services that enrich lives and drive progress across various sectors, while honoring our rich heritage. As a dynamic holding company, we're committed to bringing quality, innovation, and sustainability to every corner of India.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to creating value for our customers, partners, and communities by delivering exceptional retail experiences, supporting local artisans and farmers, and fostering a culture of sustainability and innovation. Our goal is to be a dynamic force in the retail sector, continually evolving to meet the diverse needs of modern consumers.
