At the core of Oltown's operations lies its robust wholesale staple distribution network. With deep roots in the agricultural sector, Oltown acts as a vital link between farmers and retailers, ensuring a seamless supply chain for essential commodities. By collecting and distributing staples directly from the source, Oltown guarantees the freshness and authenticity of its products while supporting local farming communities.
