FAQs

Q: What is Karunadu Products India Pvt Ltd?

A: Karunadu Products India Pvt Ltd is a holding company that manages various brands in retail, food service, and agriculture, including Oltowns supermarkets, cafes, soup outlets, and AgriLink.

Q: How can I become a franchise partner?

A: To become a franchise partner, visit our Franchise Opportunities page and fill out the inquiry form. Our team will then guide you through the application process.

Q: What franchise options do you offer?

A: We currently offer franchise opportunities for Oltowns supermarkets, cafes, and soup outlets.

Q: What is AgriLink?

A: AgriLink is our wholesale staple distribution network that connects farmers directly with retailers, ensuring fresh and authentic products.

Q: Do you have an online store?

A: Yes, we have an online store that sells handpicked, regional-specific products from different parts of India.

Q: What makes Karunadu Products India Pvt Ltd unique?

A: Our commitment to blending traditional values with modern business practices, supporting local communities, and offering diverse, quality products sets us apart.

Q: Are there employment opportunities at Karunadu Products India Pvt Ltd?

A: Yes, we're always looking for talented individuals. Please visit our Careers page for current openings.
